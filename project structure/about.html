Alumni Donate (AD for short)is an optimal platform that allows universities and other tertiary institutions stay in touch with alumni, organize alumni events, and coordinate donation and fundraising efforts. Alumni Donate helps administrators maintain alumni contact information, plan fundraising initiatives, process and track donations, and organize alumni events and reunions. Alumni relations teams and other higher education administration members can utilize Alumni Donate software to perform all tasks related to their fundraising and engagement goals. Alumni Donate shares many of the same features as CRM software, nonprofit CRM software, fundraising software, and donor management software but is tailored specifically for colleges and universities seeking to maintain alumni relations. It may also integrate with higher education student information systems.
